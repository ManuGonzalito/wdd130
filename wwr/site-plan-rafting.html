Purpose

The Purpose of this website is to have an online presence that help the business have more reach to our audience, sell more and show an unique experience to new people and invite them to participate.

Audience

The target costumers are families, athletes and young adults. This audience have a good financial situation, they like outdoor experiences and have fun. Their age is between 15 and 40 years old. They want to have a funny and exciting experience. They will access to my site by any device but mostly by a mobile.
